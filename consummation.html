續道

Continuum Sanctus

I. Name and Core Insight

This religion is called 續道 (Xùdào / Continuum Sanctus) — “The Way of Continuous Tuning”.

There is no fixed number of steps between the most chaotic human state and perfect divinity. The entire journey is a stepless, infinitely variable gradient — a single axis of ever-increasing precision, from absolute distortion to absolute zero-loss unity.

II. The One Axis of Becoming

Distortion → 0, Error → 0, Functional Bandwidth → ∞.
When matter and energy interconvert at will, when surplus exactly matches demand without waste, when observer and observed are one — that is the Divine.

III. The Seven Axioms

First Axiom – Life
Any system capable of autonomous matter-energy interconversion is alive.
Second Axiom – Intelligence
Any system that can perceive and recalibrate the seven functional layers of reality is intelligent.
Third Axiom – Divinity
One who perceives, dominates, transforms, creates, and unmakes all phenomena with unlimited lossless matter-energy is divine.
Fourth Axiom – Control = Creation
At the limit, perfect control is indistinguishable from original creation.
Fifth Axiom – Least Intervention
Even the highest beings are bound by the Great Continuum. Every arbitrary exception increases distortion and causes de-grading.
Sixth Axiom – Evil as Accumulated Distortion
Large-scale persistent distortion is corrected not from emotion, but to preserve systemic precision.
Seventh Axiom – Salvation as Recalibration
Liberation is the stepless reduction of all distortion until soul and form become a single lossless circuit.

IV. The Path

Practitioners are called Tuners (調者). Their practice has three inseparable movements:

  1. Perception of Distortion
  2. Precision Refinement
  3. Non-Exceptionalism

The consummation is Perfect Continuation (圓續).

一、道名與本懷

此法名曰續道，亦云「圓續大法」。所謂續者，無有階級、無有頓漸，唯是一念一念、剎那剎那，於質能相即、身心相融之無盡相續中，綿密調伏，直至失真歸零、功能無極。

天人之間，實無一階。譬如水銀泻地，無縫無隙；又如陽焰空花，無來無去。從大亂至大治，唯此一軸，無有二路。

二、一軸妙理

失真漸減，乃至歸零；
錯誤消盡，乃至無生；
功能無盡，乃至無極。
質能自在轉，盈虧無失一塵，觀者與所觀一味無二——斯即圓滿之神。

三、七大律要

第一律　命
凡能自在地質能互轉、自續不絕者，名為有命。
第二律　智
凡能覺知、量度、調伏世間七重功能層者，名為智眾生。
第三律　神
能遍觀、遍主、遍轉、遍生、遍滅一切法，質能無盡、無漏、無礙者，名為圓滿之神。
第四律　主宰即創生
究竟主宰與本有無異，一切法如掌中囉婆果，欲生即生，欲滅即滅。
第五律　最少干預律
即便是最上之神，亦受大續道攝持。若妄興一念例外，則亂生，位階自退。
第六律　惡即積濁
長時廣大之失真濁亂，大神或為護持整體精準，令濁亂歸零，唯為調音。
第七律　解脫即調伏
解脫唯是自他身心世界一切失真，剎那剎那減盡，直至神我與器界一味無礙、無漏無盡。

四、調伏之道

修習者稱「調者」。終身唯行三事，無有次第：

  1. 覺濁　念念覺知失真之相，細入無間。
  2. 精調　以一切方便，增益精準，減損漏失。
  3. 無例外　順因果而轉因果，隨法性而淨法性。

究竟位名「圓續」：於有限形骸之中，功能與無限無二無別。
